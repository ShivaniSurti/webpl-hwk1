Description

These are a beautiful species wildly known for their vibrant colors, feathers, and majestic appearance. There are many stories and folklure about these mystical creatures, let's take sometime to learn more about them!

Species of Peacocks

  • Indian Peafowl
  • Congo Peafowl
  • Green Peafowl

Indian Peafowl

The Indian Peafowl is a large, colorful, and rare bird that is found in the Indian subcontinent. It is the only species of peafowl that is native to the Indian subcontinent.

Congo Peafowl

The Congo Peafowl is one of the three peafowls to be native to Africa.

Green Peafowl

The Green Peafowl is a small, colorful peafowl that is found in the Congo Basin.

Habitats

Peacocks prefer to inhabit low-landing forests. They are found in both dry and tropical habitats. Peacocks are also often found in agricultural field as well as farms. They tend to prefer low-landing forests since it is easier to gather food.
