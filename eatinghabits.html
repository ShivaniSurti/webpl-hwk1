Eating Habits

What consists of a Peacock's Diet???

Let's answer your questions now!!

Peacocks generally are not picky eaters. Their diet is heavily influenced by the environment they are in and the season. Even though Peacocks (like most birds) are heavily adapted to a plant-based diet, it is surprising to know that they are actually omivores. Like I mentioned before, peacock's diet is influenced by the season and the environment. If there are not too many plants in the environment, then the peacock will eat mostly insects. Peacocks are also known to eat snakes. While snakes are a good dietary source, Peacocks do not only attack snakes for good. Peacocks actually use snakes as threat so when they do see a snake, they will attack it.

How do Peacocks Gather Food?

Like humans, peacocks use a gathering approach. Peacocks usually live in flocks. They gather around for food during the day-time and during the night, they gather in groups. Both male and female peacocks gather for food. However, during mating season, the fertile females and a couple of male peacocks stay in a safe location, while bachelor peacocks gather for food.
