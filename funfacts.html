Fun Facts

Today we will be going the top three facts about peacocks!

1. Peacocks can fly.

This may sound incorrect such their manes are so heavy, but peacocks can actually fly.

Learn More

2. Peacocks are omnivores.

Peacocks are herbivores, meaning they eat plants and other animals. They are also omnivores, meaning they eat both plants and animals. It depends on the season and food avaliability whether they eat plants or animals.

Learn More

3. Peacocks are not born with their beautiful feathers.

The beautiful colors that we all love on the peacock actually are not there from birth. Peacocks grow these colors as they get older.
