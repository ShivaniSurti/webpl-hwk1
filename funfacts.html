Fun Facts

Top 5 Facts about Peacocks:

  1. Peacocks are praised as "Snake Killers".
  2. Female peacocks are called peahens and both genders together are called peafowl.
  3. Peacocks can fly.
  4. Peacocks are not born with their beautiful feathers.
  5. Young Peacocks are called peachicks.
